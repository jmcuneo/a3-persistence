The Procrastinator's Paradise: Introducing To Not-Do List!

Drowning in to-dos? Us too. But what about the tasks that make you sweat?

Check This Out! Perks of a To-Do Not List

  • Chill Out: Free your mind by acknowledging the dreaded tasks.
  • Focus What Matters: Seeing "not-to-dos" prioritizes your actual work.
  • Embrace the Rebellion: Saying "no" is productive sometimes!

List-orical Journey: From Clay Tablets to Chill Pills

People have used lists for ages, like those old clay things and sticky notes we use today. We've always used them to remember things, even stuff we don't want to do.

The "to-do" list is pretty new, thanks to people in the 1900s who liked getting things done. But what about the stuff we keep putting off? To Not-Do List is here for that, like a special place for those forgotten tasks we don't want to think about.
